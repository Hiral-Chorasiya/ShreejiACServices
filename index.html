At Café Lumière, we believe that coffee is not just a drink — it's an experience, a ritual, a moment of warmth in a busy world. Nestled in the heart of the city, our café is a haven where artistry meets comfort.

From sourcing single-origin beans across the globe to perfecting each roast in-house, every detail is crafted with passion and precision.
